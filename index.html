Privacy Policy – PriceTrack Pro

Last updated: March 2025

1. Overview

PriceTrack Pro is a Chrome extension that tracks product prices on websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

2. Data We Collect

PriceTrack Pro stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Product names and URLs you choose to track
  • Price values and price history for tracked products
  • Your language preference (TR/EN)

This data never leaves your device. It is stored using Chrome's local storage API and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, address)
  • No browsing history
  • No financial or payment information
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No data is sent to third parties

4. Permissions Used

  • storage – To save your tracked products locally
  • alarms – To check prices periodically in the background
  • notifications – To alert you when a price drops
  • tabs / scripting – To open product pages in the background for price checking
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) – To support price tracking on any website

5. Third Parties

PriceTrack Pro does not share any data with third parties. No analytics services, advertising networks, or external APIs are used.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
